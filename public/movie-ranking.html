The Best Movies According to Amanda

My 3 favorite movies of all time

Moulin Rouge

Romantic, tragic, and a musical. Nicole Kidman and Ewan McGregor are perfectly casted in this film, and Baz Luhrmann's vibrant take on the story is magical.

Avengers: Endgame

The perfect wrap-up to a groundbreaking movie series. The battle scene when everyone arrives to take down Thanos is *chef's kiss*.

Django Unchained

One of the few 3-hour movies that I didn't want to end. Powerful storytelling, incredible acting, and a gripping plot.
